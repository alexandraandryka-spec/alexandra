Memory from my stay in South Africa

My stay in South Africa in 2013 was one of those journeys I still remember vividly. It was a time of intense observations, encounters with extraordinary nature and a daily reality very different from the one I knew before.

One of the places that made a particularly strong impression on me was Kirstenbosch, one of the nine National Botanical Gardens managed by SANBI in South Africa.

Kirstenbosch is the oldest and largest botanical garden in South Africa and the third-largest botanical garden in the world. An article inspired by that visit was later published in the journal Wszechświat.
